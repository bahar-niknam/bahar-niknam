# About Me

I am Bahar Niknam, with a background in Mathematical Statistics and research interests in deep learning and computer vision. My recent work has focused on uncertainty-aware learning, transformer-based architectures, and medical image analysis.
I am particularly interested in understanding how deep neural networks learn from limited or uncertain data, and how statistical methods can improve the reliability and efficiency of modern AI systems.
